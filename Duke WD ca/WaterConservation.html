Why Water Conservation Matters

Water conservation involves the careful management of water to reduce waste and ensure its availability for future generations. While 71% of the Earth's surface is covered with water, only 0.5% is accessible freshwater. This makes conservation efforts critical in combating water scarcity, which affects over 2 billion people worldwide.
